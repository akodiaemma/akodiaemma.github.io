About Crossgates Connect

Crossgates Connect is a verification infrastructure layer built to support export readiness and market access. Its role is to help structure fragmented SME business, compliance, operational, and supporting sector records into a more usable supplier identity.

Through its verification layer, Crossgates helps turn scattered readiness efforts into buyer-ready, verification-ready Trade Passports.

Leadership

We are planners, engineers, logisticians, and coders united by a singular mission: to replace supply chain fragmentation with institutional clarity. By integrating human expertise with AI-enabled verification, we bring intelligence and momentum to Uganda's most complex trade corridors.

Problems We Solve

Many SMEs are prepared through different programmes, institutions, associations, and support systems.

Yet supplier readiness often remains fragmented across paper files, emails, PDFs, spreadsheets, and isolated records.

Crossgates Connect helps solve this gap by structuring supplier identity, compliance records, operational evidence, and supporting sector information into a clearer, buyer-ready view. This helps turn fragmented readiness into stronger commercial trust.
