STIV Framework

Structured Trade Infrastructure & Verification

STIV is not just software—it is a discipline. It represents our commitment to moving trade from "hope-based" to "evidence-based."

Uganda's production potential is vast, but the gap between the farm gate and the global buyer remains wide. For too long, small and medium enterprises have struggled with fragmented documentation and a lack of digital visibility, making them "invisible" to international procurement networks.

CrossGates was built to solve this "missing link." We provide the digital and physical verification layer that turns raw supply into audit-ready, buyer-readable assets.

Why Verification Matters

Many SMEs already have some level of readiness work, documents, or support behind them. The challenge is that this information is often scattered across separate files, systems, institutions, and informal exchanges.

Crossgates adds value by working as a verification layer above these fragmented records.

How Crossgates Fits

Crossgates does not replace traceability systems, standards bodies, sector support programmes, or export-readiness efforts. It complements them by helping convert existing readiness inputs into a more structured, buyer-ready, verification-ready supplier view.

This is how Crossgates helps make readiness more commercially usable.
