Our Purpose

Making Readiness Commercially Usable

Many SMEs are prepared through different programmes, institutions, associations, and support systems. Yet supplier readiness often remains fragmented across paper files, emails, PDFs, spreadsheets, and isolated records.

Crossgates Connect helps solve this gap by structuring supplier identity, compliance records, operational evidence, and supporting sector information into a clearer, buyer-ready view.

This helps turn fragmented readiness into stronger commercial trust.

How It Works

Three Steps to a Trade Passport

Crossgates Connect works as a verification infrastructure layer that helps turn fragmented supplier readiness into a clearer, more commercially usable supplier identity. It does this by bringing together supplier business, compliance, operational, and supporting records, assessing their readiness status, and structuring them into a buyer-ready, verification-ready Trade Passport.

01

Supplier Onboarding

Crossgates begins by onboarding the supplier and capturing core business, operational, product, and contact information. This creates the foundation for a structured supplier identity.

02

Verification & Readiness Review

Crossgates reviews the supplier information and supporting records to identify what is present, what is missing, what is incomplete, and what may be expiring or require follow-up. This helps create a clearer view of supplier readiness.

03

Trade Passport Creation

Once the information has been structured and assessed, Crossgates converts it into a Trade Passport — a buyer-ready, verification-ready supplier identity. The Trade Passport helps present supplier readiness in a clearer and more trusted format.
