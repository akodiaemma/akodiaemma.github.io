Our Purpose

Making Readiness Commercially Usable

Many SMEs are prepared through different programmes, institutions, associations, and support systems. Yet supplier readiness often remains fragmented across paper files, emails, PDFs, spreadsheets, and isolated records.

Crossgates Connect helps solve this gap by structuring supplier identity, compliance records, operational evidence, and supporting sector information into a clearer, buyer-ready view.

This helps turn fragmented readiness into stronger commercial trust.

The Problem

Supplier readiness remains fragmented across paper files, emails, PDFs, spreadsheets, and isolated records — even after training and support.

The Gap

Existing programmes prepare suppliers — but the resulting readiness is difficult to present in one trusted, commercially usable view.

The Crossgates Role

A verification infrastructure layer that structures fragmented readiness into a buyer-ready, verification-ready Trade Passport.

Three Steps to a Trade Passport

Crossgates Connect works as a verification infrastructure layer that helps turn fragmented supplier readiness into a clearer, more commercially usable supplier identity.

01

Supplier Onboarding

Crossgates begins by onboarding the supplier and capturing core business, operational, product, and contact information. This creates the foundation for a structured supplier identity.

02

Verification & Readiness Review

Crossgates reviews the supplier information and supporting records to identify what is present, what is missing, what is incomplete, and what may be expiring or require follow-up.

03

Trade Passport Creation

Once the information has been structured and assessed, Crossgates converts it into a Trade Passport — a buyer-ready, verification-ready supplier identity.
