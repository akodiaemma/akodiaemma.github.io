The Pilot

Pilot & Priority Sectors

Crossgates Connect begins with disciplined, sector-based pilots designed to test its verification infrastructure in real conditions.

Uganda's Wider Ecosystem

Crossgates is designed to complement Uganda's wider ecosystem for agricultural commercialisation, value addition, export readiness, trade facilitation, private-sector competitiveness, and market access.

It does not replace the work of ministries, associations, standards bodies, exporters, or market-support programmes. It works above and alongside them by making supplier readiness more structured, more visible, and more commercially usable.

Why Sector Focus Matters

Crossgates does not treat SMEs as one generic group. Different sectors face different documentation needs, readiness gaps, standards expectations, commercial requirements, and market-entry conditions.

A sector-based pilot approach allows the Crossgates verification infrastructure to be tested more carefully, aligned more practically, and improved more intelligently before wider rollout.

This is why Crossgates begins with one selected sector at a time. It keeps pilot execution disciplined, practical, and evidence-driven.
