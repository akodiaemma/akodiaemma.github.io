What We Do

The Verification Layer for Global Trade

Crossgates Connect works as a verification infrastructure layer that turns fragmented supplier readiness into a clearer, more commercially usable supplier identity.

How It Works

Many SMEs already have some level of readiness work, documents, or support behind them. However, this information is often scattered across separate files, emails, and informal systems.

Crossgates adds value by working above these fragmented records—structuring them into a buyer-ready, verification-ready Trade Passport.

The 7-Step Readiness Pathway

A structured process from supplier onboarding to a buyer-ready Trade Passport.

01

Supplier Onboarding

We begin by onboarding the supplier and capturing core business, operational, product, and contact information to create the foundation for a structured supplier identity.

02

Record Collection

Crossgates gathers key supporting records—including business documents, compliance materials, and operational evidence—moving readiness out of scattered files into one structured process.

03

Verification & Readiness Review

We review the captured information to identify what is present, missing, incomplete, or expiring. This creates a transparent view of supplier readiness.

04

Commercial Readiness View

We organize the supplier into a usable view, highlighting strengths and identifying gaps. This makes readiness easier to understand for institutions, banks, and market participants.

05

Trade Passport Creation

Once structured and assessed, the information is converted into a Trade Passport—a buyer-ready supplier identity built for commercial trust.

06

Validity & Gap Visibility

Crossgates maintains active visibility into missing or expiring records, ensuring that supplier readiness is not a static snapshot but a continuous, practical pathway.

07

Market-Ready Supplier View

The result is a supplier profile that is easier to assess, easier to trust, and easier to use in serious international market engagement.
