What We Do

More Than Scholarships

The Scholarship Ministry has evolved from simply awarding scholarships to building a comprehensive educational and workforce preparation program for students and families in the Abyssinian community.

🎓

Preparing Competitive Scholarship Applicants

Helping students navigate scholarship applications and financial aid to maximize their opportunities.

💼

Career Readiness & Professional Success

Resume development, interview training, mentorship, and career exposure for real-world readiness.

🤖

AI Fluency for Students & Seniors

Intergenerational digital literacy and responsible AI education for our entire community.

📈

Wealth & Financial Literacy

Education around investing, entrepreneurship, and homeownership to build generational wealth.
